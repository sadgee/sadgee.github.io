[ 01 ] About

Analyst by trade.
Storyteller by instinct.

I came to data through teaching. For five years before CMU, I worked with hundreds of students helping them make sense of their performance — and I learned that the hardest problem in data isn't the analysis, it's making the output mean something to the person who needs to act on it.

That's the thread running through everything I've built since. At Deloitte, I built a hybrid ML + GenAI decision intelligence platform using Claude Sonnet via AWS Bedrock — but the part I'm proudest of is the transparency layer that made the model legible to non-technical executives.

At Gilead, I built risk-scoring models for a Phase III clinical trial across 32 global sites — but what mattered was equipping the operations team with the dashboards they needed to act in time.

Today I'm finishing my MS in Business Analytics at CMU Tepper and looking for full-time roles in Data Analytics, Business Analytics, or Consulting starting May 2026.
