Case 1 · Network Design
CaLNG — LNG Distribution Network Design
Evaluated three distribution strategies for California LNG peak-shaving: slow-buildup satellite tanks, centralized storage, and a hybrid model. Used Monte Carlo simulation to assess take-or-pay contract value (mean demand 144 MMcf/day, SD 59 MMcf/day) and NPV analysis at 15% discount rate.
